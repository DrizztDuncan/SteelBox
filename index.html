STEEL BOX

CONDITION

NEW BOXES
Typically, “new” boxes have traveled the ocean 1 time – to get here where you can purchase it. Overall the condition is like-new with working doors, seals, no holes, or leaks. These boxes may have minimal to no rust, dents, dings.Delivery is typically 14-21 days from the date of purchase.
NOTE: New boxes are available in gray, beige or tan. Color requests may proling the delivery process.
USED BOXES
Used boxes are typically between 8-18 years old and have traveled the ocean on average 30-70 times. Every used box will have varying levels of visible surface rust. If having rust on your Used box is a concern, you might consider purchasing a New/One-Trip box instead. Rust on a Used box will not generally be a problem if the box is properly maintained and managed. Pressure washing and painting your box will help to extend the life of your box.
Note: We don't guarantee box colors.

CUSTOMER PICK UP

Customer has a truck & trailer that allows them to pick up their box from the depot on their own and will not be charged for delivery. A representative from our Corporate Office will contact customer within 1 business day to confirm pick up information; including address and any additional information to complete pick up process.

CUSTOMER UNLOAD

Our experienced Logistics Team will set up a driver with a 4×4 truck and 40’ Hotshot trailer to pick up your box and deliver it to your property. The customer will be required to have the appropriate heavy equipment available on-site to anchor the box. Acceptable heavy equipment will need to weigh at least as much as the box itself. The Customer unload is perfect for customers that are concerned about having a Semi-truck on their property, have a narrow entrance, or require more maneuverability and may have an additional fee.

ROLL-OFF DELIVERY

Our experienced Logistics Team will set up a driver with a Roll-off trailer to pick up the box and deliver it to your property. The Roll-off trailer is required if the customer does not have the proper heavy equipment needed to assist with the unload process. Or in a situation where they do not have room for the standard delivery, possibly due to needing an exact location for their box. This will also be required for any boxes that have had fabrication work done. This delivery will automatically add an additional fee to the delivery cost.
